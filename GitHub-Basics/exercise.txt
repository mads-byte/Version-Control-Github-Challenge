 Explain what the terminal is in your own words
 It is a way of communicating with your computer locally.

 What is the major difference between Git and GitHub?
 Git works on your local machine while GitHub operates online.
 
 What are the steps to fork a GitHub repository?
 First, go to the URL of the target repository
 Second, click on the 'fork' button on the top, right hand side of the screen
 Lastly, when you land back on your personal space on Github, at the top, you should see your username/"name-of-the-repo-you-forked-from"
